CSS Sticky Footer

Finally, a flexible box model CSS sticky footer.

A CSS flexbox sticky footer that just works...

... in just a few browsers. But hey, that's cool, right? We've all tried to use a sticky footer one time or another, but they never seem to come out right, do they? Well, the days of a hard to understand CSS-based sticky footer are thankfully over. In just a few simple lines of CSS3 with no extra HTML markup, I've fashioned a sticky footer. It's been tested in IE 5 and up, Firefox, Safari and Opera. And it... fails in IE5-IE9 and Opera. For now at least. ;-)
