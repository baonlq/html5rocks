Preface

New Day, New Possibilities

Greetings friends, developers, pioneers and dreamers to the 12th Edition of Bert Appward’s Field Guide to Web Applications. Whether your inclination is to dip a toe or to dive headfirst into creating great web applications, I hope you’ll find the information and exercises contained within these pages to be a useful guide on your journey.

Only now has the technology truly caught up with the passion for an immersive web app experience.

Why Now?

Previously the promise of web apps was hampered by technical limitations and fell short of the traditional desktop user expectations. But today, with modern browsers supporting features like HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript and more, the horizon has been broadened. Robust, feature-rich web apps that offer feature parity to their native or desktop equivalents are are now not only possible but expected by users.

Right Now!

This field guide is designed to help you identify web applications, how to spot the great ones, but most importantly, how you can build a great one of your own. The demand for apps is strong, and it’s coming from everywhere, don’t miss out on this important revolution.
